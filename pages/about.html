Our Story

It all started in 2018 while in University where we were studying Software Engineering in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia. At first, we had only been acquainted with each other because we had the same classes. As students we were given group assignments and projects that required the participation of all the members. Contrary to popular belief, those things were done by a few, leaving us in a dilemma. Actively participate in those projects which meant sacrificing the social life aspect of higher education institutes vital to the improvement of our social skills or ignore the responsibility of doing those works which would definitely leave us short-handed as Engineers. By deciding to come together as a group we chose the best of both worlds. What looked like a minor decision at that moment turned out to be a vital turning point in all of our lives. While working together, we came to realize each other’s strong and weak points, motivating each other to cross our limits.The work was divided to all of us, creating a system of check and balance and also making it easier for each of us to find our niche. It created an atmosphere where everyone takes turns at leading, It let us be able to work as a unit but also be responsible for each other’s work. What we did for school and how we did it became the foundation for what we do now.

Our Goals and Values


Growth

Growth is one of our main focus. As Ethiopia and Africa are rapidly growing their roots in the technology sector, We aim to be a part of the generation that aids to achieve the transformation into better ways while working to keep our valuable traditions intact.

Innovativeness

With the lack of a well resourced infrastructure and awareness of the uses of modern technology being one of the many potentional set-backs, we aim to use that not as a excuse but an oppurtunity to be creative in our solutions.Finding new ways to implement results and keep the momentum going.

Vision

Our vision is to become a forefront in the Ethiopian and African Development game. We aim to achieve our goals while doing our part to inspire the younger generation to reach for theirs.

Team Work

As it has been said multiple times,"Team Work Makes The Dream Work". We came together with the understanding that it is always better to be working as a group to achieve common goals.
